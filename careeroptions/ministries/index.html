Ministries and Their Functions

Ministry of Finance

  • Manages national budget, revenue collection, and public expenditures.
  • Develops financial policies and regulations.
  • Oversees taxation and fiscal policies.
  • Supervises banking and financial institutions.

Ministry of Education

  • Formulates educational policies and standards.
  • Oversees primary, secondary, and higher education institutions.
  • Implements educational reforms and programs.
  • Promotes research and development in education.

Ministry of Health

  • Develops and implements health policies and programs.
  • Manages public health services and healthcare facilities.
  • Conducts health awareness and disease prevention campaigns.
  • Oversees medical research and healthcare workforce training.

Ministry of Defense

  • Manages national defense and armed forces.
  • Develops defense policies and strategies.
  • Oversees military operations and defense infrastructure.
  • Ensures national security and defense preparedness.

Ministry of Home Affairs

  • Manages internal security and public order.
  • Oversees police and law enforcement agencies.
  • Handles immigration, border control, and citizenship.
  • Implements disaster management and emergency response.

Ministry of Foreign Affairs

  • Manages international relations and diplomacy.
  • Represents the country in international organizations and forums.
  • Oversees embassies and consulates abroad.
  • Negotiates treaties and agreements with other nations.

Ministry of Justice

  • Develops and enforces legal policies and frameworks.
  • Manages the judiciary and legal system.
  • Oversees correctional facilities and rehabilitation programs.
  • Ensures the protection of human rights and the rule of law.

Ministry of Agriculture

  • Develops agricultural policies and programs.
  • Supports farmers and agricultural research.
  • Oversees food security and rural development.
  • Promotes sustainable farming practices.

Ministry of Labor and Employment

  • Develops labor policies and regulations.
  • Promotes employment and job creation.
  • Oversees labor welfare and workers' rights.
  • Implements vocational training and skill development programs.

Ministry of Environment

  • Develops environmental policies and regulations.
  • Oversees conservation and natural resource management.
  • Conducts environmental impact assessments.
  • Promotes sustainable development and pollution control.

Ministry of Transport

  • Develops transportation policies and infrastructure.
  • Oversees road, rail, air, and maritime transport.
  • Implements traffic safety and regulation measures.
  • Promotes public transportation systems.

Ministry of Energy

  • Develops energy policies and regulations.
  • Oversees electricity generation and distribution.
  • Promotes renewable energy and energy conservation.
  • Manages national energy resources and infrastructure.

Ministry of Water Resources

  • Manages water resources and irrigation systems.
  • Develops water policies and conservation programs.
  • Oversees water supply and sanitation services.
  • Implements flood control and water management projects.

Ministry of Culture

  • Promotes cultural heritage and arts.
  • Supports cultural events and activities.
  • Oversees museums, libraries, and cultural institutions.
  • Develops policies for cultural preservation and promotion.

Ministry of Tourism

  • Develops tourism policies and strategies.
  • Promotes the country as a tourist destination.
  • Supports tourism infrastructure and services.
  • Implements tourism marketing and promotional campaigns.

Ministry of Information and Broadcasting

  • Develops media and communication policies.
  • Oversees public broadcasting and media regulation.
  • Promotes access to information and digital literacy.
  • Manages government communication and public information.

Ministry of Commerce and Industry

  • Develops trade and industrial policies.
  • Promotes international trade and export.
  • Supports industrial development and investment.
  • Oversees market regulation and consumer protection.

Ministry of Science and Technology

  • Promotes scientific research and innovation.
  • Develops policies for science and technology advancement.
  • Supports technological development and commercialization.
  • Oversees national research institutions and projects.

Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs

  • Develops housing policies and urban planning.
  • Oversees urban development and infrastructure projects.
  • Promotes affordable housing and urban renewal.
  • Manages public housing and urban services.

Ministry of Rural Development

  • Develops rural development policies and programs.
  • Supports rural infrastructure and services.
  • Promotes rural employment and livelihoods.
  • Implements poverty alleviation and social welfare projects.

Ministry of Social Welfare

  • Develops social welfare policies and programs.
  • Supports vulnerable and marginalized groups.
  • Oversees social security and welfare services.
  • Promotes inclusive and equitable development.

Ministry of Sports and Youth Affairs

  • Develops sports policies and programs.
  • Promotes youth development and engagement.
  • Supports sports infrastructure and training.
  • Organizes national and international sports events.

Ministry of Telecommunications

  • Develops telecommunications policies and regulations.
  • Oversees telecommunication infrastructure and services.
  • Promotes digital connectivity and broadband access.
  • Supports innovation in telecommunications technology.

Ministry of Public Works

  • Develops infrastructure policies and projects.
  • Oversees construction and maintenance of public infrastructure.
  • Manages transportation and public facilities.
  • Implements urban and rural infrastructure development.

Ministry of Immigration and Citizenship

  • Manages immigration policies and services.
  • Oversees border control and visa regulations.
  • Promotes citizenship and integration programs.
  • Handles refugee and asylum services.

Ministry of Fisheries

  • Develops fisheries policies and regulations.
  • Supports sustainable fishing practices.
  • Oversees aquaculture and marine resource management.
  • Promotes seafood industry and trade.

Ministry of Women and Child Development

  • Develops policies for women and child welfare.
  • Supports programs for women's empowerment and child protection.
  • Oversees nutrition and early childhood development.
  • Promotes gender equality and family welfare.

Ministry of Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs)

  • Develops policies for SME growth and development.
  • Supports entrepreneurship and innovation.
  • Provides financial assistance and market access for SMEs.
  • Promotes skill development and capacity building for SMEs.

Ministry of Interior

  • Manages internal security and public safety.
  • Oversees law enforcement and emergency services.
  • Develops policies for crime prevention and control.
  • Ensures national stability and public order.

Ministry of Innovation and Digital Economy

  • Promotes digital transformation and innovation.
  • Develops policies for the digital economy and e-governance.
  • Supports technology startups and digital entrepreneurship.
  • Oversees digital infrastructure and services.
