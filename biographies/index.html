Inspiring Entrepreneur Biographies

Just Dial Founder - VSS Mani

VSS Mani is the visionary founder of Just Dial, an online local search engine that revolutionized the way people find and connect with businesses in India. He built Just Dial from scratch and transformed it into a multi-billion-dollar company. Mani's dedication and innovation in developing this platform have earned him immense recognition in the business world.

To read more about VSS Mani and his incredible journey, visit this link: VSS Mani Biography.

Adda247 Founder - Anil Nagar

Anil Nagar is the inspiring founder of Adda247, an e-learning platform that provides quality education and test preparation for various competitive exams. Starting from just Rs. 1 lakh and fueled by his determination and passion, Anil Nagar has grown Adda247 into a prominent company with a valuation of over Rs. 1400 crore. His journey from an IIT aspirant to a successful entrepreneur has motivated millions of students across the country.

To know more about Anil Nagar's life and achievements, read this captivating article: Anil Nagar Biography.
